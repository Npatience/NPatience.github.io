About

C-LINK APP is a mobile application that is going to enable patients to book appointments at their convinience, it is going to enable them know when the specialists are available hence saving them the burden of going to hospitals and the doctors are not around.This app is also going to enable doctors to keep track of the appointments for the day to avoid inconviniences in one way or the other.
